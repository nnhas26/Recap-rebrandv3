VOL. 5 - Industry 5.0

Summary: Currently, the entire world community is welcoming the 5.0 industry. Joe Vasquez said that industry 5.0 is an era where people are used to working with robots and smart machines. Industry 4.0 is an era where technology such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and so on are used to bring intelligence and automation into the manufacturing industry and everyday life. In short, industry 5.0 will shift mechanical tasks to robots, while the creative tasks are left to humans.

Products such as smart-watch, smart-TV, or even smart-lamp are artificial intelligence technologies. As technology has been very influential in everyday life, industry 5.0 is slowly shifting some technical or mechanical work to be done by robots. Roles that were originally performed by humans, can be performed by robots that have been installed with artificial intelligence. Meanwhile, humans will take over the creative tasks which are difficult for artificial intelligence to understand. Humans are required to be more creative in making products than routine work.

In fact, creativity is not easy to automate, no matter how hard researchers are trying. Artificial intelligence is only able to help humans accelerate their creativity, in other words, AI is more of a tool than a party involved in Industry 5.0. This does not apply to all sectors because many sectors still depend on human expertise or intelligence.


Date: 8th October 2020
Speaker: Joe Vasquez
